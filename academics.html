Balancing School and Basketball

Education and passion can go hand in hand.

Balancing academics and basketball is not easy, but it’s possible with discipline. I make sure to manage my time, finish school requirements early, and still dedicate hours to training. Basketball motivates me to do well in school, because I know both are essential for my future.
